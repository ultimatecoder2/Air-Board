Now Don't limit your Creativity.

Air-Board brings you an online virtual whiteboard, where you can write on your screen, without the need of any specific hardware.

How to Use

You can use Air-Board using 3 types of finger gestures.

Write

For writing/editing, you need to use your index finger

Make Selection

To select different brush color, eraser, or for freehand movement on screen, you can use 2 fingers

Clear Canvas

You can erase complete content on canvas, by raising your 4 fingers

AirBoard Canvas

Show your creativity. Let's make it real.

Air-Board

Air-Board uses computer vision to detect hand gestures. On the basis of user's hand movement, canvas is updated and user's content is visible. Currently, Air-Board supports writing, and erasing. More features will be added soon in future. For using Air-Board, you must provide access to webcam.
